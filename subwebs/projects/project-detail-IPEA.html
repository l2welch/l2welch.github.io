Design Engineering - Solidworks Projects

In the winter of 2024/25 I worked at an engineering firm which specialized in B2B furniture production and distribution. The position was located in an industrial suburb of Milan, Brianza. As a second generation Italian, I worked with 100% Italian speaking engineers and put my communication skills to the test.

My role at the firm was predominantly modelling button panels, fasteners, brackets, and chair feet on Solidworks, and then creating technical drawings which were used by other companies to access detailed information on product specifications. I also contributed to designing the layout for the “Salone del Mobile, Milano”, an upcoming furniture design conference in the design capital of the world. In order to get the job, I proved to my employer that I was capable of learning professional level Solidworks independently. I did an online course, everyday completing three hours of work for three months, with the hope that it would land me the job. When I was hired, my hard work was demonstrated when I was able to design complex organic shapes in a more efficient way than my colleagues.

By the end of my time, I modelled 50+ button panels, brackets, and fasteners, and took part in the manufacturing process, from concept, to communicating with clients, and finally to realization.
