LivinStudios: My Small Pottery Business

A small ceramics business I founded in the 10th grade with the intention of not only selling my pieces, but also connecting with the pottery community. I hand-make all kinds of ceramic objects from an endless quantity of mugs, to spoon-rests, and even teapots. Most of my work is sold through custom commissions and farmers markets.

Beyond being a business, it is a platform for me to share my passion. Much of my work has been driven by personal exploration—projects that I find exciting or meaningful, such as making 25 trophies for a local boating competition or creating a memorial vase for a friend who passed away. The business has been a way to connect with my community and share something that I am passionate about.
