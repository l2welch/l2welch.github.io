Private Innovation in the Public Interest: Research Assistant at UofT

How can we reimagine corporate responsibility to genuinely serve the public good? For one year, I had the privilege of assisting Professor Anita McGahan in exploring how corporate leaders can effectively collaborate with government to drive meaningful social change.

As a research assistant, I focused on producing and publishing podcasts, leading discussions and setting agendas in meetings, and training the team to eventually take over my responsibilities.
