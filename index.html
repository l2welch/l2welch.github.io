About Me

Hi! I'm Livia Welch, a first-year Mechanical Engineering at the University of Waterloo with a passion for blending technical problem-solving with creativity. My experiences range from working in Italy designing CAD models with a 100% Italian team, to developing workflows and managing podcast production as a Research Assistant, and even teaching skateboarding!

I thrive in environments where I can combine engineering precision with artistic design, whether that’s prototyping ideas, crafting ceramics, or building community projects like Foodbox.to. Organized and entrepreneurial, I enjoy creating structure in teams while bringing energy and originality to problem-solving.

My long-term goal is to become an industrial designer who merges sustainability, art, and engineering—developing products and systems that are both functional and environmentally conscious. In the short term, I’m seeking opportunities to grow as a designer and engineer in settings where creativity and technical skill come together to make an impact.
